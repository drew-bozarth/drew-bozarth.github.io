Dungeonborne

Won the "Indie-Spirit" award and was selected as a finalist at the GameSig Intercollegiate Computer Game Competition 2024!

Game Description:

  • Dungeonborne is a 2D rougue-like dungeon-crawler farming RPG hybrid, where players cultivate a farm, manage their inventory, engage with town NPCs, purchase and equip stronger gear, and conquer multiple dungeon floors fighting various enemies and encountering bosses along the way.
  • The game features a dungeon with inifinite floors, scaling in difficulty, and challenging players with a randomly generated layout of rooms to escape each floor.
  • Players progress by defeating these bosses, acquiring higher level items, and continually advancing deeper into the dungeon.
  • Features a completely original soundtrack!
