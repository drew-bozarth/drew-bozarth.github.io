Chat Noir

This is a game I developed during my internship at Galaxy SDK.

App Description:

Solve the mystery!

Unravel the mystery, discover hidden clues, and outsmart AI suspects in Chat Noir. The future of detective games is here!

Dive deep into the world of Chat Noir, a thrilling murder mystery game, where your detective skills are put to the ultimate test. Engage with hyper-realistic characters who are suspects in a series of mind-bending murder cases. Each suspect has a unique personality, secrets to protect, and stories to tell. As you delve into the rich narratives, remember that every choice you make and every question you ask can bring you one step closer to uncovering the truth—or veer you off track.

FEATURES:
Immersive Storytelling: Intricate murder cases, each with its unique narrative and cast of AI suspects.
AI-powered NPCs: Engage in realistic conversations with our AI-powered characters. They can be your friend, foe, or wild card—depending on how you play.
Deep Interactions: Develop relationships with characters. Your conversations, choices, and interactions will determine their behavior towards you.
Dynamic Dialogue: No two conversations are the same. The AI responses evolve based on your interrogation style.
Real-time Clue Analysis: Analyze evidence in real-time using your virtual detective toolkit. Piece together the puzzle to find the murderer.
Multiple Endings: With multiple potential culprits and endings, you can replay the game and have a completely different experience each time.

As a detective in Chat Noir, you're not just playing a game—you're stepping into a riveting, interactive crime novel that unfolds according to your decisions. Uncover motives, decode alibis, find inconsistencies in stories, and, ultimately, unmask the killer. The truth is there, hidden in the shadows of AI interactions.
