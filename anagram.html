Anagram

This is a game I worked on during my internship at Galaxy SDK. I performed QA testing, bug fixing, and added exciting new features to the gameplay.

App Description:

Unleash your inner wordsmith in Anagrams! Beat your friends in this high-speed word puzzle game that tests your vocabulary & strategy. Ready, set, word-craft!

Welcome to "Anagrams," your new favorite word game challenge! Based on classic games like Scrabble and Words With Friends, Anagrams takes the word puzzle genre to new heights, combining thrilling gameplay with brain-boosting fun.

In this high-speed word search game, each player is given a pool of random letters and just 60 seconds to form as many words as possible. With the clock ticking, this anagram game tests not just your vocabulary, but your quick-thinking skills as well! Each letter used scores points based on its Scrabble value, rewarding strategic thinking and a wide vocabulary. Can you craft the perfect word to earn a hefty bonus?

Compete in this word scramble game against global opponents or challenge your contacts and friends in a battle of wits. Anagrams employs a turn-based system, ensuring the excitement never ends as you alternate back and forth in this engaging word race.

As an asynchronous multiplayer game, Anagrams offers the flexibility to play at your own pace. No need to synchronize with your opponent’s schedule - you make your move when it suits you. Perfect for those who enjoy strategic gameplay and language-based puzzles.

Whether you're a word search enthusiast, a Scrabble aficionado, or a fan of crosswords, Anagrams promises to deliver the challenge you're looking for. With endless possibilities and unpredictable opponents, every game is a fresh linguistic challenge. It’s time to put your vocabulary to the test in this engaging wordplay showdown!

Download Anagrams now, and immerse yourself in the exhilarating world of word puzzles! Word crafting has never been this exciting. Happy anagram solving!
